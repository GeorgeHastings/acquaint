Lists

  • An Engineer Assesses Your Home’s Flood Resiliency

    A qualified engineering firm will take measurements of your home to assess its strength and resistance to flooding, and will issue you an elevation certificate. This comprehensive audit will make clear which resiliency measures are most appropriate for your home.

  • A Guide Helps You Explore Your Options

    After the audit, a resiliency guide will come to your home to explain what it all means for you. In that conversation, the guide will outline your options, explain tradeoffs, and show you personalized financial strategies around retrofitting.

  • A Customized Resiliency Plan is Created for You

    You will be provided with a plan for your home that outlines the options that fit you best. This report can be shared with contractors, insurance agents, and others who will be helping to make your home more flood resilient for the future.
