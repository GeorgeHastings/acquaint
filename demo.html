What to do next

Making your home as flood resilient as possible means having a personalized plan that fits both your budget and your lifestyle. The Home Flood Assessment program aims to offer just that: personalized flood resiliency assistance for low- to middle-income New York City homeowners. All services are completed by vetted professionals, and are offered completely free of charge---including an elevation certificate valued at $800.
